About Me

Drawing from my extensive experience at KPN, DPG Media, Ahold-Delhaize, and Sissy-Boy, I bring valuable insights in product management and coaching. My diverse background spans both B2B and B2C environments, allowing me to adapt methods to various business models and customer types.

My approach is rooted in modern product management principles, focusing on creating products that are not just viable, but valuable, usable, and feasible. I believe in the power of effective discovery to drive innovation and create products that truly resonate with customers.
